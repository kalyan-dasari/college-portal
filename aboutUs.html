About Us - Malla Reddy College of Engineering (MRCE)

Malla Reddy College of Engineering (MRCE) is a premier institution located in Hyderabad, dedicated to providing quality education and fostering innovation in the field of engineering and technology. Established with the vision of becoming a center of excellence, MRCE offers undergraduate and postgraduate programs across various disciplines.

Our college is equipped with state-of-the-art laboratories, modern classrooms, and a comprehensive library. We emphasize not only on academics but also on the overall development of students by encouraging participation in extracurricular and co-curricular activities.

MRCE has a team of experienced faculty members who are committed to guiding students towards achieving their career goals. The institution maintains strong industry partnerships, ensuring that students gain practical exposure and are prepared for the dynamic professional environment.

Our mission is to nurture young minds, instilling in them the values of integrity, excellence, and innovation. We strive to create an ecosystem where students can explore their potential and become leaders in their respective fields.

Join us at MRCE and be a part of an inspiring journey towards academic and professional excellence.
